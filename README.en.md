# ♔ King Chess Zero

> **A modern, ad-free chess experience built for everyone.**

Play chess anywhere, anytime — with instant offline support, seamless synchronization, and zero distractions.

---

## ✨ Features

* 🚫 **Zero Ads**

  * No banners.
  * No popups.
  * No interruptions.

* 📴 **Offline First**

  * Install as a Progressive Web App (PWA).
  * Play completely offline.
  * Your games are stored locally.

* 🔄 **Auto Sync**

  * Changes are synchronized automatically whenever you're back online.

* ↩️ **Undo / Redo**

  * Navigate through moves effortlessly.
  * Review and analyze your games at your own pace.

* ⚡ **Fast & Lightweight**

  * Instant startup.
  * Optimized for desktop and mobile.

* ♟️ **Pure Chess**

  * Focus on the game—not advertisements or unnecessary clutter.

---

## Philosophy

King Chess Zero follows a simple philosophy:

> **Your chess. Your device. Your control.**

No advertisements.

No unnecessary distractions.

Just chess.

---

## Technology

* Progressive Web App (PWA)
* Offline-first architecture
* Background synchronization
* Responsive UI
* Local-first data storage
